More About Sarah (she/they)

I’m a community-focused, thoughtful, and diligent human. That’s why when I left a nearly 10-year career in communications to pursue software development, I applied for Ada Developers Academy. The organization’s mission is to change the face of tech by opening its doors to women and gender expansive adults.

During my time at Ada I have learned backend and frontend development, as well as many computer-science fundamentals. Software development isn’t so different from communications. Both involve keeping an end-user in mind, working across multiple stakeholders to fulfill needs, and having a keen eye for detail. I love learning new skills and concepts.

Along my journey, I’ve also found that the world of tech doesn’t have to be stereotypically cold or heartless. On the contrary, I have met many others who, like me, want to create community. I’d love to connect with you, too.
